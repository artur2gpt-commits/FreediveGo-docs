# FreediveGo Overview

## What is FreediveGo

FreediveGo is a global freediving platform.

The platform combines:

- Spots
- Instructors
- Schools
- Tours
- Chambers
- Community
- Ratings
- Training tools

into a single ecosystem.

---

## Mission

Make freediving information accessible worldwide.

Help divers:

- Find locations
- Find instructors
- Learn safely
- Travel easier
- Share experience

---
